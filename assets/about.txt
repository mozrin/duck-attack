Duck Attack
Version 1.0.0

A frantic game about feeding ducks while protecting Grandma.

Developed by Mozrin, Will, and Antigravity.
Built with Flutter & Flame.

Instructions:
- Drag to move Grandma to the bench.
- Tap to throw bread crumbs.
- Don't let the ducks peck you!
- Feed them until they leave, or stun them to escape.
